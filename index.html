Who Am I, and Why Programming?

Welcome to my Program Portfolio! My name is Anna, I am lucky enough to reside in the beautiful State of Washington, and am currently attending programming classes in Seattle at a school known as Epicodus. This page is where all of my previous and future programming projects can be found. I am 24, and just developed a rather intense interest for coding over a very short period of time. After having no real knowledge about any of the "behind the scenes" work in computer programs, I found myself chatting with my father (He works in the programming/coding feild himself) about the subject whilst I was trying to determine what path I might want to take in life, what career, what school, what interest? Intigued by the idea, and curious about what all went into this, I couldnt help but wonder, and I couldnt possibly write something off that I'd never experienced, i had to find out what it was all about! So that led me to a workshop run by Epicodus, and after that, it was settled. Completely enthralled by the problem solving and creative aspects, I managed to sign up for the classes Epicodus held, and started less than a week later. It's the threshold of an adventure into the world of web development, computer languages, frameworks, etc. and im eager to learn more and hopefully write some amazing programs!
